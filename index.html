Tourist Attractions

Boma National Park

Boma National Park is located in eastern South Sudan, close to the Ethiopian border. It is known for its stunning wildlife and beautiful landscapes.

Bandingilo National Park

Bandingilo National Park is home to a diverse range of wildlife species and is known for its annual migration of antelopes.

Southern National Park

Southern National Park is a vast area with diverse ecosystems, including savannahs, forests, and wetlands.

Shambe National Park

Shambe National Park is located in central South Sudan and is known for its diverse wildlife.

Lantoto National Park

Lantoto National Park is located in southwestern South Sudan and consists of forests and open glades.

Nimule National Park

Nimule National Park is located in the Eastern Equatoria region of South Sudan, known for its savanna woodland and diverse wildlife.
